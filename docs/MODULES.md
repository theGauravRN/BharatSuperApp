# Bharat Super App — Feature Modules

Reference list of the 13 product modules from the Product Vision. Use this for roadmap and implementation planning.

| # | Module | Description |
|---|--------|-------------|
| 1 | **Social Profiles & Communication** | Public profiles, follow/friend, feed, stories, groups, DMs, E2E encryption |
| 2 | **Audio & Video Calling** | 1:1 & group calls, screen share, live stream, WebRTC + mediasoup |
| 3 | **News & World Updates** | 22 languages, AI translation, personalized feed, fact-checking, audio news |
| 4 | **FinTech & Payments** | UPI, QR, micro-loans, BNPL, micro-insurance, savings, investments, bills |
| 5 | **Agri-Tech** | Mandi prices, weather, crop advisory, pest/disease AI, soil health, schemes |
| 6 | **Hyperlocal Commerce & Delivery** | Kirana onboarding, 30-min delivery, B2B, reseller, ONDC, voice search |
| 7 | **EdTech 2.0** | Govt exams, vocational skills, vernacular courses, job-linked certs, scholarships |
| 8 | **HealthTech for Bharat** | Teleconsultation, ABDM, diagnostics, medicine delivery, AYUSH, mental health |
| 9 | **Creator Economy & Short Video** | Short video, monetization, live gifting, vernacular discovery, duet/collab |
| 10 | **EV & Green Tech** | EV charging map, fleet management, EV loans/subsidies, solar marketplace |
| 11 | **GovTech** | DigiLocker, land records, grievances, scheme checker, voter info, RTI |
| 12 | **Gig Economy & Jobs** | Blue-collar matching, skill verification, income smoothing, worker benefits |
| 13 | **Vernacular AI Assistant** | Voice in 22 languages, intent detection, context-aware, offline basics |

## Phase 1 (Months 1–6)

- Vernacular AI Assistant + Social + Messaging
- Audio/Video calling (WebRTC)
- News in top 5 languages
- Target: 1M users, 3 states

## Phase 2 (Months 7–12)

- UPI + Bill payments
- Hyperlocal commerce + Kirana
- Agri-Tech (Mandi prices)
- All 22 languages
- Target: 10M users

## Phase 3 (Months 13–18)

- HealthTech, EdTech, Gig Economy, Creator/Short Video
- Target: 50M users, pan-India

## Phase 4 (Months 19–24)

- Mini-app marketplace, GovTech, EV/Green Tech, Lending/Insurance
- Target: 100M+ users
